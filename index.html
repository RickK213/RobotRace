ROBOT RACE

Welcome to Robot Race—a Javascript Dice Game.

How to Play:

  1. The goal of the game is to be the first player to build their robot. Each robot has 10 pieces.
  2. To begin the game, each player rolls the 6-sided die. The higher number goes first.
  3. In each round of play, each player rolls the 4-, 8-, 12- & 20-sided dice.
  4. If the result of the roll is a PRIME number, the player has the opporunity to add to their robot by rolling the 10-sided die.
  5. Each piece of the robot is numbered 1-10. The roll of the 10-sided die determines which piece the player adds to his robot. If the player already has that piece, nothing is added and their opponent takes a turn.

Let the race begin!
